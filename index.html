About Nam Snout Project

We are a non-profit dedicated to rescuing cats and dogs in Namibia and finding them loving forever homes.

"Name your Adoption Fee"

This is a unique concept, inspired by Woof Project in Cape Town, whereby there is no set adoption fee. We provide a guideline, but how much you donate towards the adoption of your new furry family member, is your decision.

Our adoption donation covers a check up, the dewormer, two vaccinations and sterilization.

Even if you are not adopting, any donation big or small will enable us to continue saving lives.
